01. Background
What has been the journey and Where are we now ?

Describe in brief, the journey of the brand till now. It's brief history, milestones, key events and where the brand is right now. Also, tell us about the competition which you are chasing.


02. Target Audience
Ultimately, the point of communications is to get people to do things.

Which people? What things? This is the most important part of the brief. Explain your target audience. Who is/can be the most motivated to buy/ recommend your product? Tell us more about them...


03. Background
What should advertising do for your brand?

Please help us understand your brand-related issues that you would like the campaigns to address. We will be happy to discuss this and help fill it together.
